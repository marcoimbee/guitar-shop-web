Custom Guitar Shop: a guide for the user

About this website:

CustomGuitars.com is a website where the user can buy all the latest guitars and basses the
music industry has to offer.


How to use this website

You can navigate through the website by using the buttons in the top menu.
You can reach the homepage, the products tab, the login paghe and your personal page.

Choosing the products you like:

In the products tab you will be able to choose and add them to your cart in case you wanted to purchase them.
To be able to do this, you have to be logged in to your account.
You can use filters to adjust the products you are interested in.

Registering and logging in:

If you are still not registered to this website, you will have to register in order to log in.
Follow the links in the login page and insert your data in the fields.
You will have to choose also a username and a password.
Once you will be registered, insert your username and password to login.

Checking out:

Navigate to your personal area page to take a look at which instruments you added to your shopping cart.
You will still be able to remove them in case you wanted to.
In order to checkout, add your credit card data by following the links in your profile area.
Insert all the asked data and come back to your profile page. Once done, click the 'Checkout' button to buy your items.
The items will be shipped as soon as possible to your address.


Enjoy yor stay!
